Your Questions, Answered

Is this really confidential?

+

Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of our service. We use enterprise-grade, HIPAA-compliant technology and adhere to strict internal SOPs. What you share with your therapist stays between you and your therapist.

Who are the therapists?

+

All of our therapists are fully qualified professionals with a minimum of a Master's degree in Psychology or a related field. They are rigorously vetted and undergo continuous training through our in-house Lakeside Health Academy to ensure the highest standard of care.

What if I don't connect with my therapist?

+

The therapeutic relationship is key to success. If for any reason you don't feel your therapist is the right fit, you can easily and confidentially switch to another professional on our platform at no extra cost. We ensure a smooth transition; our secure backend technology and internal handover processes allow your new therapist to be brought up to speed confidentially, so you can pick up right where you left off without having to repeat your entire story.
